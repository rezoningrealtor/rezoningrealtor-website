Thinking about buying in the Tri-Cities? Explore each city — neighbourhoods, transit, parks, schools, and what makes each one unique.

🏙️

Coquitlam

The largest of the three, with SkyTrain access, Burke Mountain new builds, and one of Metro Vancouver's most multicultural communities.
