My Story

Real Estate Decisions Deserve More Than Just Opinions

I did not start in real estate.

My career began in consulting — working across four continents including India, the Americas, Europe, and Africa — helping organizations navigate complex decisions where the stakes were high and the answers were not obvious. What that taught me was simple: ask the right questions first, understand the full picture, and don’t make assumptions. That mindset shapes how I approach Real Estate today.


Coquitlam is not just where I Work — it’s Home!


I have lived in Coquitlam for over 20 years. My kids grew up here. I’ve seen this market evolve through different cycles—not just as a professional, but as a homeowner and part of the community. That perspective matters when you are making decisions that affect your future.


A Different Perspective Into Real Estate


I did not come into Real Estate through sales. I came through development. This meant being directly involved in sourcing land, working with builders, navigating zoning changes and approvals. Not only did I thoroughly enjoy it, this gave me a clear understanding of how value is actually created and where things can go wrong. Because sometimes, a property’s value isn’t just in the home—it’s in what can be built on it.


How I Work


Understand the situation first. Look at the numbers realistically. Then make a recommendation that makes sense. Not every deal is a good one—and I will tell you that when it is not.


At the end of the day


This is not just about buying or selling. It’s about making the right decision with one of your most important assets.
