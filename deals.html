How It Works

About the Weekly Deal

Every Monday I post one property that caught my eye — not because someone paid to feature it, but because I genuinely think it represents value. Here's how it works:

1
I find the property
I scan MLS, my network, and off-market sources every week for properties that are undervalued, overlooked, or represent a specific opportunity — development potential, below-market pricing, etc.
2
I explain why it's interesting
Not just the listing details — I walk through the specific angle: what the developer sees, what the buyer gets, what the number actually means in today's market.
3
You reach out if it fits
No pressure, no obligation. If the deal looks interesting, contact me and I'll give you the full picture — including anything that could be a downside. Honest analysis, always.
