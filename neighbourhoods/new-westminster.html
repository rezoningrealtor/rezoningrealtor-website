About New Westminster

New Westminster — "The Royal City" — is BC's oldest incorporated city, and one of the region's best-kept real estate secrets. With direct SkyTrain access on the Expo Line, a revitalized waterfront along the Fraser River, and a thriving Uptown commercial district, New West punches well above its humble price tag.

Buyers who discover New Westminster often stay — the character homes in Glenbrooke North and Brow of the Hill are genuinely charming, while the Quay area delivers waterfront condo living at a fraction of what similar units cost in Vancouver.
